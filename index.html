Simple Chakhchoukha Recipe

A traditional Algerian dish, perfect for hearty meals. This recipe combines tender lamb or chicken stew with homemade flatbread pieces, creating a flavorful and satisfying dish.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 500g lamb or chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chickpeas, soaked overnight (or use canned chickpeas)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 liter (4 cups) water or broth
  • 2 green chilies (optional)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

1

Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until the onion becomes translucent.

2

Add the lamb or chicken pieces to the pot and cook until they are browned on all sides.

3

Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, ground cumin, paprika, ground cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes start to break down and the spices become fragrant.

4

Add the soaked chickpeas to the pot and pour in enough water or broth to cover all the ingredients. If using canned chickpeas, you can add them later in the cooking process (about 20 minutes before the stew is done).

5

If you prefer some heat, you can add chopped green chilies to the stew at this point.

6

Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the meat is tender and the flavors have melded together. Stir occasionally and adjust the seasoning if needed.

7

Once the stew is ready, remove it from the heat and garnish with freshly chopped coriander.
